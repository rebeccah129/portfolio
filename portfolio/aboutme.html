Rebecca Ho is thirteen years old and about to attend Fairview High School. In middle school she has attended many clubs, NetZero, Animal Action, and Track. Her favorite pastime is photography and enjoys taking pictures of animals,plants, and landscapes. She dislikes roaches and sometimes her brothers.
